Explore Ranking Styles

Discover journal rankings based on our unique weighting methodologies:

Equal Contribution

Treats all citation types equally, with self-citations (Weight: 1), internal citations (Weight: 1), and external citations (Weight: 1). This provides a completely balanced view of journal impact.

Self (1) Internal (1) External (1)

Balanced Impact

Emphasizes a balanced view of journal impact, considering self-citations (Weight: 1), internal citations (Weight: 2), and external citations (Weight: 3). Provides a graduated assessment of citation value.

Self (1) Internal (2) External (3)

External Influence

Prioritizes external influence with reduced weight for self-citations (Weight: 0.5), while valuing internal (Weight: 2) and external (Weight: 3) citations. Emphasizes broader academic impact.

Self (0.5) Internal (2) External (3)
